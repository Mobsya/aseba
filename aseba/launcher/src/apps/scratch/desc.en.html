Scratch allows you to create interactions between the real world, with Thymio, and your screen using the Scratch "Scene" and "Sprites" system.

For example, you can create an interactive story, or even a Thymio controlled video game. Creativity is the only limit.

  • Create interaction between your robot and your computer screen
  • Drag and drop blocks of text to program Thymio and the Scratch sprites
  • No need of an internet connection

Important: keep the wired version of Thymio plugged in to your PC when you program with Scratch. Actually Wireless Thymio robot is the best choice with Scratch.

Find tips and more information clicking on the help (?) button.

Visit www.thymio.org to get learning material and ideas about how to use Thymio in your classroom.
