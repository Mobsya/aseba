Take a first step into a more challenging programming syntax including more computer science concepts.

  • Suitable for children who can read and are more familiar with computers
  • First step from visual to textual programming language
  • Drag and drop blocks of text to program Thymio
  • More possibilities than in VPL in a programming logic similar to Scratch
  • Can be used to teach or discover concepts of computer science
  • No need of an internet connection

Find tips and more information clicking on the help (?) button.

Visit www.thymio.org to get learning material and ideas about how to use Thymio in your classroom.
