Aseba Studio

What is Aseba Studio?

Aseba is a complete text programming language that will allow you to have a precise control on everything inside your Thymio robot. You will be able to get a direct feedback of the sensors and plot their values in real-time.

How does it work?

Write your program in the editable zone in the middle. You can use the auto-completion or the pre-written keywords to help you. When you are done, press the Stop, then the Run button to send the instructions to Thymio.
