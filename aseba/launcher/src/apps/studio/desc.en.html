Aseba is a complete text programming language for robots that will allow you to have a precise control on everything inside your Thymio robot

  • Discover text programming
  • Get the full control of your Thymio robot
  • Get real-time graphs of what is recorded by the sensors
  • Connect several Thymio robots to one dongle and make them interact together
  • No need of an internet connection

Find tips and more information clicking on the help (?) button.
